 The movie "Encanto" follows a story of the Madrigal family. They are an extraordinary family who live hidden in the mountains of Colombia with each member famed and respected for their gifts. The magic of the Encanto has blessed every child in the family with a unique gift except Mirabel. However, she soon may be the Madrigals' last hope when she discovers that the magic surrounding the Encanto is now in danger.

 A sequel to the previous movie "Puss in Boots", we continue on the journey of our main character, Puss in Boots. He discovers that his passion for adventure has taken its toll when he learns that he has burnt through eight of his nine lives. Like a proud knight taken off his high horse, he learns the lessons of humility and appreciation of his mortality. So, Puss sets out on an epic journey to find the mythical Last Wish and restore his nine lives.
